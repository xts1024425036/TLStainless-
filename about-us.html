About Us

    Beijing Tianlang Intelligence Vally Teconology Co.,Ltd supplies a range of industrial products that covers lifting, lashing, slings, rigging, marine, itowing, transportation, construction, fishing and security applications for many years.

    We had exported our products to Europe, America, Africa, Australi and New Zealand… etc. Our customers cover more than 70 countries and regions worldwide.
